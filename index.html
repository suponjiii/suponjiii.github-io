How it's going to work

  1. You contact me through one of the methods mentioned below
  2. If you’re willing to pay, it’s basically guaranteed I accept, if not I’ll have to see if I have time! BUT PLEASE TELL ME IF YOU PAY OR NOT BEFOREHAND!
  3. If I accept, you give me the song, the off vocal and if you already have them, your lines. Tell me additional things (effects etc.) too!
  4. Also, if you pay you can set a deadline so I won’t be lazy!
  5. I will send an invoice with a partial payment before I start. Upon payment, sit back, enjoy and wait till I’m done
  6. I’ll send you in a Critmix (unmastered and Preview-Quality) in which I have done most of the things...In that mix, you can tell me which things to change (Volumes, effects, timing, everything basically)
  7. You happy? Then I am happy. Full mix, Upload and possibly payment!
